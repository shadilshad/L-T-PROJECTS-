About Low Bucket

Low Bucket is a growing brand under the I.DAD Group, created by Mr. Dilshad VV from Kerala, India, with the vision of offering premium-quality dates, nuts, and exotic spices at prices everyone can afford. Based in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, Low Bucket focuses on delivering big choices at small prices, making high-grade varieties like Ajwa, Majdool, Sukkari, Ruthab, and carefully selected spice blends accessible to every customer.
